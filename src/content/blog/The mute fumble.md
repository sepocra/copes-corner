The first play of the game

The ball is passed their way. Then the fumble.

Not always a turnover and sometimes recoverable.

Were they already thinking about what was to come next?

That first play doesn't go unnoticed.

The call starts, the question is out there. Then the mute fumble.

A soft apology appears. The tech gets blamed.

The initial momentum is lost. The conversation continues.

Some notice the moment was imperfect, preparation was missing and the presence was elsewhere.

The first possession reveals something. Both on the call and in the game.
