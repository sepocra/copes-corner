---
title: Time doesn't always feel the same
description: Some moments crawl and others disappear
pubDate: 2026-05-15
---
A minute is always a minute.
It just doesn’t always feel like it.

The last few minutes of a one-sided game. Waiting for the toaster to finish. Kids putting on their shoes when you're already late.

Some moments crawl when you're paying attention to every second.

Then there are the moments where time disappears entirely.

You're immersed in what's in front of you. The people, the activity, the conversation. You stop checking the clock because it stops mattering.

Everyone gets the same 1440 minutes in a day. No one knows how many days they get.

Maybe a good life counts the great moments when you stop counting.
