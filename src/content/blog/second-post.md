Unexpected change (and some expected change) can be tough to navigate.

- Spending time to appreciate the achievements and experiences prior the change
- Taking the time to acknowledge the new reality 
- Talking with people you trust outside of the change

These help me find the best way through a change.

Thanks Edoardo Pallaro for the visual reminder that in every change there is always a chance to reframe it in a positive way. Sometimes it is hidden right in front of you and needs to be highlighted.
