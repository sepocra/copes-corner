title: The meeting starts when
description: Each warm-up is different
pubDate: 2026-07-03

The meeting says 10am start.

Our routines before shape the meeting.

Every player enters the stadium at different times. A time that suits their ritual. A time that gives them their warmup. Ready for tip-off.

The coffee before joining. The email we tried to send first. The last call we're still in. These shape the warm-up.

The meeting starts at 10. But we aren't always ready to start at 10.

The mute fumble gives it away. Asking for something to be repeated. Half in the meeting, half somewhere else. The wrong screen being shared.

The prep doesn't show up on the agenda. It shows up in the room.
